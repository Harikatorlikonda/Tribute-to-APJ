Awards and Achievements

Dr. Kalam was honored with prestigious awards such as Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan for his remarkable contributions to science and the nation.

About Him

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, also known as the "Missile Man of India," was a visionary scientist, teacher, and the 11th President of India. His contributions to science and technology, particularly in India's missile and space programs, remain unparalleled.

Born on 15th October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he rose from humble beginnings to become a national hero. Dr. Kalam's life serves as an inspiration to dream big and work hard to achieve those dreams.
